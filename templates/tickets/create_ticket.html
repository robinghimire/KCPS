❓ Common Questions

▶ How long does it take to get a response?

Response times depend on priority: Urgent (1-2 hours), High (4-6 hours), Medium (1 day), Low (2-3 days).

▶ Can I update my ticket after creating it?

Yes! You can view and add notes to your ticket anytime from your tickets page. Just click on the ticket to view its details and add updates.

▶ What information should I include?

Include a clear description, error messages, steps to reproduce, what you've already tried, and any relevant attachments or screenshots. The more context you provide, the better we can assist you.

▶ How do I track my ticket status?

After submitting your ticket, you'll be able to track its status in real-time. You'll receive email updates whenever there's progress on your ticket.
